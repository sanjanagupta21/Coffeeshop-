About Us

Serving Since 2024

Our Story

Welcome to Moon Mocha, where our passion for exceptional coffee and creating meaningful connections come together.

Our coffee shop was born from a dream to provide a space where every cup tells a story and every visit feels like coming home.

More than just a place to enjoy coffee, Moon Mocha is a community hub where friendships are brewed alongside the perfect cup of coffee. Join us and become part of our story, where every moment is savored and shared.

Our Vision

At Moon Mocha, we are passionate about delivering the finest coffee experience. Discover our premium coffee beans sourced from the best regions worldwide. Whether you love bold espressos, smooth medium roasts, or exotic single-origin brews, we have something for every palate.

We source our beans from the finest growers worldwide, ensuring quality and sustainability in every batch. Our skilled baristas craft each cup with care, offering a diverse menu that caters to all tastes.

Our Services

Fresh & Organic Beans

Fastest Door Delivery

Whether you're at home or at the office, our dedicated delivery team ensures that your favorite brews and treats arrive at your doorstep piping hot and fresh. Simply place your order online and enjoy the convenience of Moon Mocha's exceptional coffee delivered straight to you in no time. Because great coffee waits for no one!

Fresh Coffee Beans

At Moon Mocha, freshness is at the heart of every cup we serve. We source our coffee beans from the best growers around the globe, ensuring they are harvested at their peak ripeness. Once they arrive at Moon Mocha, our beans are carefully roasted in small batches to preserve their natural flavors and aromas.

Best Quality Coffee

At Moon Mocha, we believe that every cup of coffee should be an extraordinary experience. That’s why we are committed to serving only the best quality coffee. From the moment the beans are harvested to the final pour, we prioritize quality and sustainability in every step of the process. Our skilled baristas take pride in crafting each cup with precision and care, delivering a rich, flavorful coffee that delights the senses.

Online Table Booking

At Moon Mocha, we strive to make your coffee experience as convenient as possible. With our online table booking service, reserving your favorite spot at our coffee shop has never been easier. Whether you're planning a catch-up with friends, a cozy date, or a productive work session, simply visit our website or use our app to book your table in advance. Enjoy the comfort of knowing your spot is waiting for you, so you can relax and savor every moment at Moon Mocha. Book your table online today and let us take care of the rest!
